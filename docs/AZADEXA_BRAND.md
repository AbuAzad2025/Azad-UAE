# Azadexa

**Azadexa** (Arabic: **أزاديكسا**) is the product identity for the intelligent multi-tenant ERP and commerce platform built by **AZAD Intelligent Systems**.

## Product definition

Azadexa is not a single online store. It is a multi-tenant ERP SaaS platform for managing business operations, commerce, accounting, inventory, warehouses, sales, purchases, customers, suppliers, payments, tenant stores, branches, and platform-owner workflows.

## Core positioning

**Azadexa — Intelligent ERP & Commerce Platform**

Alternative extended positioning:

**Azadexa — Multi-Tenant ERP Cloud for Commerce, Accounting & Operations**

## Platform scope

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Tenant-specific business data isolation
- Sales and purchases management
- Inventory and warehouse management
- Accounting and ledger workflows
- Customers and suppliers management
- Branch and user management
- Payments and platform-owner operations
- Tenant store capabilities
- Public landing, donation, and package purchase flows
