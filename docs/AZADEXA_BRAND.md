# Azadexa

**Azadexa** (Arabic: **أزاديكسا**) is the product identity for the intelligent multi-tenant ERP, accounting, inventory, and commerce platform built by **AZAD Intelligent Systems**.

The name must be used as a system name with meaning, not as a random marketplace-style word.

---

## Name meaning

**Azadexa** is positioned as:

```text
AZAD + EXA
```

Where:

- **AZAD** connects the product to **AZAD Intelligent Systems**.
- **EXA** represents the platform's operating themes:
  - **E**nterprise operations;
  - business e**X**change between sales, purchases, payments, stock, and accounting;
  - intelligent **A**utomation across ERP workflows.

So the practical brand meaning is:

```text
Azadexa = AZAD Enterprise Exchange & Automation Platform
```

Arabic brand meaning:

```text
أزاديكسا = منصة أزاد لتشغيل الأعمال، تبادل العمليات، الأتمتة، المحاسبة، المخزون، والتجارة.
```

This keeps the name connected to the system content: ERP, accounting, inventory, payments, tenant stores, and platform-owner workflows.

---

## Product definition

Azadexa is not a single online store. It is a multi-tenant ERP SaaS platform for managing business operations, commerce, accounting, inventory, warehouses, sales, purchases, customers, suppliers, payments, tenant stores, branches, and platform-owner workflows.

## Platform scope

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Tenant-specific business data isolation
- Sales, POS, and returns management
- Purchases and supplier management
- Inventory, warehouses, serials, warranty, and costing
- Accounting, general ledger, journal entries, financial dimensions, and reports
- Customers, suppliers, partners, branches, and users
- Tenant store capabilities
- Store checkout, coupons, loyalty, variants, reviews, and notifications where enabled
- Platform-owner payment vault, donations, package purchases, and owner operations
- API, GraphQL, monitoring, WhatsApp, gamification, and AI surfaces where enabled
