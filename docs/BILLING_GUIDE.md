| Method | Processing | Settlement |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Bank transfer | 3–5 business days | Manual reconciliation |
| Credit / debit card | Instant | Stripe integration |
| Cryptocurrency (NOWPayments) | 10–60 minutes | Automatic |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Instant | Stripe (roadmap) |

## 4. Billing Cycle

| Aspect | Rule |
|--------|------|
| Invoice date | 1st of every month |
| Payment due | Net 14 days |
| Late fee | 0.05% per day after due date |
| Suspension | 30 days after due date |
| Reactivation | Full payment + 50 AED reactivation fee |

## 5. Annual Commitment

- Annual plans receive 2 months free (17% discount).
- Annual plans can be paid in 2 installments: 50% upfront, 50% after 6 months.
- Cancellation of annual plan: prorated refund if cancelled within 30 days. No refund after 30 days.

## 7. Refund Policy

| Scenario | Refund |
|----------|--------|
| Cancellation within 48 hours of first payment | 100% |
| Cancellation within 14 days | 50% |
| Service unavailable > 24 hours in a month | Prorated credit |
| Duplicate charge | 100% |
| Downgrade mid-cycle | Prorated credit applied to next invoice |

## 8. Tax

| Jurisdiction | Tax | Application |
|--------------|-----|-------------|
| UAE | 5% VAT | Added to all invoices |
| Saudi Arabia | 15% VAT | If customer is KSA-based |
| Palestine | Exempt | If customer is Palestine-based |

Tax registration number (TRN) must be provided for tax-exempt eligibility.

## 9. Invoice Delivery

- Invoices are emailed to the billing contact on file.
- PDF copies are available in `routes/owner/settings.py` → Billing.
- WhatsApp invoice delivery is available on Professional and Enterprise.
