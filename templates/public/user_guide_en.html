Sales Management

How to Create a New Invoice

  1. From the sidebar, select "Sales"
  2. Click "New Invoice"
  3. Choose customer (or add new)
  4. Add products (price appears automatically based on customer type)
  5. Select payment method
  6. Save invoice
Tip: Use the smart filter to quickly search for customers!

Customer Management

Add New Customer

  1. Go to "Customers" from menu
  2. Click "New Customer"
  3. Enter basic information (name, phone, email)
  4. Set customer type (retail, wholesale, VIP)
  5. Save

Customer Statement

View complete financial transactions for any customer, including all invoices and payments.

Products Management

Add New Product

  1. From sidebar, select "Products"
  2. Click "New Product"
  3. Enter product details
  4. Select category
  5. Set prices (purchase and sale)
  6. Set stock alert level
  7. Upload product image (optional)
  8. Save

Product Categories

Organize products into categories for easy searching and filtering.

Warehouse Management

Inventory Tracking

The system automatically tracks inventory with every:

  • Sale invoice (decreases stock)
  • Purchase invoice (increases stock)
  • Stock movement (manual adjustment)
Important: Low stock alerts help you reorder before running out!

Reports & Analytics
