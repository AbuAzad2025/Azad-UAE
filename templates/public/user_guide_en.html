Overview

This guide reflects the current, real system capabilities and avoids exposing any operational secrets.

  • Sales, purchases, products, customers, suppliers.
  • Warehouses, stock movements, branch-level operations.
  • Payments, receipts, checks, returns.
  • General Ledger (GL) and financial reporting.
  • Tenant storefront and online order flow.
  • AI assistant and operational insights.

Access & Roles

Login Modes

  • Company login: for tenant users and branch users.
  • Platform login: for platform owner/developer accounts.

Data Isolation

Each company operates within its own data scope. Cross-tenant access is restricted by policy and role checks.

Security note: never share privileged credentials and avoid storing passwords in plain text.

Daily Operations

Sales Workflow

  1. Select customer.
  2. Add products and quantities.
  3. Apply discounts/tax/shipping if needed.
  4. Select payment method and save.
  5. Print or share invoice output.

Purchasing Workflow

  1. Create purchase invoice linked to supplier.
  2. Update landed costs and stock quantities.
  3. Track supplier balances and payments.

Finance & Accounting

  • General Ledger entries and account tracking.
  • Receipts/payments with settlement history.
  • Incoming/outgoing check lifecycle management.
  • Receivables and payables monitoring.

Currency & Exchange Rate

Multi-currency operations are supported, including live exchange rate display in the navbar with user-selectable currency pairs.

Inventory & Branches

  • Single or multi-warehouse setup.
  • Stock transfers between warehouses.
  • Low-stock monitoring and replenishment flow.
  • Branch-level visibility and operational scope.
Tip: schedule recurring stock checks per branch to reduce variance.

E-commerce Store

  • Each tenant can run a dedicated storefront (slug-based).
  • Online orders are created as `online_store` sales.
  • Platform owner can centrally lock/unlock tenant stores.
  • Store payment methods are managed from platform controls.

Reports & Analytics

  • Operational dashboards for daily/monthly tracking.
  • Sales, purchases, inventory, and customer insights.
  • Financial reports including GL-oriented outputs.
  • Storefront order and conversion visibility.

Admin & Configuration

  • Enable/disable modules (sales, purchases, inventory, reports, AI, e-commerce).
  • Set default language, timezone, and currency.
  • Manage users, roles, and permissions.
  • Configure branding and invoice templates.

Backups and recovery flows are available to authorized users only. Keep backups in a secure location and test restore procedures periodically.

Best Practices

  • Apply least-privilege access for all users.
  • Separate daily operator accounts from high-privilege admin accounts.
  • Review audit/security logs regularly.
  • Validate storefront behavior after major settings changes.
  • Run period-end checks for stock and finance before close.
